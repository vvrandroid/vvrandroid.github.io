Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Expense Note collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application. We take your privacy seriously. Your personal expense notes and entries are stored securely on your device or in your personal cloud storage (e.g., Google Drive) and are NOT accessed, collected, or stored by the developer.

Note for Google Drive Users: Our app uses the drive.file scope to allow you to back up your data. We only access the specific backup files created by this app and cannot see or modify any other files in your Google Drive.

1. Data Collected By The App Developer

The app collects the absolute minimum information necessary for core features (sign-in and subscriptions) to function.

Data Type How Collected Purpose
Google User ID Automatically via Google Sign-In To create a secure account and link your subscription history.
Google Account Email Automatically via Google Sign-In To verify identity, authenticate subscriptions, and send account-related alerts.
Purchase History Processed via Google Play To verify subscription status and manage renewals or cancellations.

2. Information NOT Collected or Stored

  • User-Generated Content: Your daily expense notes, entry details, and categories remain on your device or your personal Google Drive.
  • Sensitive Financial Data: We do not receive or store your credit card or full payment information; this is handled exclusively by Google Play.
