Privacy Policy for Slide Volume

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Slide Volume collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application to manage your device audio and volume settings.

Local Privacy: Your volume preferences and app configurations are stored locally on your device. We do NOT use cloud storage (such as Google Drive) for your settings, and we do not host your personal configuration data on our own servers.

1. Data Collected By The App Developer

We collect only the essential data required to manage your account, verify premium memberships, and ensure the app functions correctly across your devices.

Data Type Source/SDK Purpose
Google Account Email & ID Google Sign-In To create your account, identify your membership status, and provide access to the Deletion Portal.
Purchase History RevenueCat & Google Play To validate your subscription status, unlock Pro features, and manage billing history.
Device Metadata System API Basic info (model, OS version) to ensure the volume control interface and overlays are compatible with your specific hardware.

2. Information NOT Collected or Stored By the Developer

To ensure your privacy, we do NOT collect or have access to:

  • Audio Data: We do not record, listen to, or process any audio from your microphone or speakers.
  • Media History: We do not track what music, videos, or media you are playing while using our volume sliders.
  • Location Data: We do not track your physical location.
  • Cloud Backups: Unlike our other apps, Slide Volume does not request access to your Google Drive.

3. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard partners to provide advertising and membership management. These partners collect data according to their own policies.

A. Google AdMob (Advertising)

AdMob may collect device identifiers (like Advertising ID) and IP addresses to serve relevant ads and prevent click fraud. You can reset your Advertising ID in your Android system settings.

B. RevenueCat (Membership Management)

We use RevenueCat to process and track in-app purchases reliably. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier and your purchase history to ensure your "Pro" features work across all your devices.

C. Google Play Billing

All financial transactions are handled by Google. We never see or store your credit card or bank details.

4. Data Rights and Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Request a permanent deletion of your account and associated sign-in metadata via our Account Deletion Portal.
  • Clear all app settings at any time by clearing the app data in your Android System Settings.

5. Contact Us

For support or privacy inquiries: vvrandroid@gmail.com
