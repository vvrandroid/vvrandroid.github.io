Delete Account and Data

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Deep Battery or Expense Note, please follow the instructions below.

Request Deletion

Please send an email from the account you wish to delete to:

vvrandroid@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - [App Name]

Upon receiving your request, we will remove your account identifiers, historical logs, and purchase links from RevenueCat within 7 business days.

What happens next?

  • Your Google Sign-In profile will be unlinked.
  • All app-specific data (battery logs or expenses) will be permanently purged.
  • Note: You must manually cancel any active subscriptions in the Google Play Store app to stop future billing.
