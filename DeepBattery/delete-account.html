Account and Data Deletion Request

App Name: Deep Battery

Developer: VVR Android

How to delete your account:

If you signed in using your Google Account and wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to vvrandroid@gmail.com.
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Deep Battery".
  3. Include the Google email address associated with your account.

Requests are typically processed within 7 business days.

What data will be deleted?

  • Your user profile and Google Sign-In identifiers.
  • All historical battery tracking logs associated with your account.
  • Your RevenueCat subscriber identity and purchase history logs.

What data is retained?

We may retain certain transaction records for legal, tax, or fraud prevention purposes as required by law. Please note that deleting your account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. You must cancel active subscriptions via the Google Play Store app.
