Account & Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your Deep Battery account and all associated tracking data, please follow these steps:

How to Request

Send an email from the Google account you used to sign in to:

vvrandroid@gmail.com

Subject: Deep Battery Account Deletion

We will verify your request and purge all battery logs, account identifiers, and RevenueCat records within 7 business days.

Important Notes

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Deleting your account does not cancel active Google Play subscriptions. You must do this in the Play Store app settings.
